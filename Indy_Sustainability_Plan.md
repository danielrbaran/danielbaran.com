## üéØ **THE CHALLENGE: MENTAL HEALTH & COMMUNITY REBUILDING**

### **The Honest Reality:**

Daniel has never put enough effort into community and relationships in his entire life. The increased clarity of mind from recovery has revealed why:

**Every person on earth has a default label in his mind of "threat."**

His brain assumes everyone wants to hurt him, either directly or by taking something from him that he can't afford to give. It takes a LOT to convince that protection infrastructure that someone is not a threat, so very few people have ever made it through.

**This means:**
- Building relationships and community is brutally hard
- The sheer amount of energy needed to disable all the defenses can be exhausting
- Normally calculates that there are other "more important" things to spend energy on
- **But he wants to change this. He really does.**

### **The Strategy: Start with Accessible Entry Points**

Rather than trying to force relationships in high-defense situations, focus on activity-based and interest-based communities where defenses can naturally lower:

1. **AA Community** - Natural community, already comfortable, knows he can be comfortable in Indianapolis
2. **Maker Community** - Activity-based, shared passion (carbon fiber, Linotype machines, old school machining), less threatening because it's about the work, not personal connection
